How It Works

Three steps to better dates

No more writing your own bio. Let the people who actually know you do the talking.

1

Your friend pitches you

A friend signs up as your "pitcher" and creates a profile for you — your bio, photos, and what makes you great. They know the real you.

2

They swipe on your behalf

Your pitcher browses profiles that other friends have created and picks the ones they think you'd click with. Think of them as your personal matchmaker.

3

You go on the date

When there's a match, your pitcher breaks the ice and sets up the date. You show up, meet someone great, and thank your friend later.

FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

How does Pitcher work?

A friend signs up as your "pitcher" and creates a dating profile for you. They write your bio, pick your photos, and swipe on potential matches on your behalf. When there's a match, your pitcher chats and sets up the date. You just show up.

Can I see my profile before it goes live?

Yes! Your pitcher creates your profile and submits it for your approval. You review everything — bio, photos, prompts — and only when you approve does it become visible to others.

What if I don't like what my friend wrote?

You can request changes before approving your profile. Communication with your pitcher is key — they want to represent you well, so give them feedback.

Can I be both a pitcher and get pitched?

Absolutely. You can pitch your friends while also having a friend pitch you. Many users play both roles.

Is Pitcher free?

Pitcher is free to use. We may introduce optional premium features in the future, but the core experience will always be free.

What about privacy and safety?

Your data is stored securely. You can block or unmatch anyone at any time. We require phone verification and friend connections to reduce fake profiles. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

When is the app launching?

Pitcher is currently in closed beta testing on iOS. Join the waitlist and we'll let you know when we're ready for the next wave of testers. Android is planned for a future release.
